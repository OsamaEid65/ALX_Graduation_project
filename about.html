About Hotel Kempinski

Kempinski Hotels, Europe's oldest luxury hotel group, traces its rich history back to 1897 when it was founded in Berlin, Germany. The brand quickly gained recognition for its commitment to exceptional service, elegant accommodations, and iconic locations.

In its early years, Kempinski established itself as a pioneer in the hospitality industry, setting new standards for luxury and sophistication. With its prestigious reputation, the hotel attracted elite guests, including royalty, celebrities, and dignitaries from around the world.
